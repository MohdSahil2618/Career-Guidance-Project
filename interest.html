COURSE DETAILS: MECHATRONICS ENGINEERING

WHAT IS MECHATRONICS ENGINEERING ABOUT?

**Mechatronics Engineering in India** is an interdisciplinary field that combines mechanical engineering, electronics, computer engineering, and control engineering. It focuses on the design and manufacture of intelligent products and processes, integrating sensors, microprocessors, and actuators. It's a field at the forefront of automation, robotics, and advanced manufacturing.

This hands-on course emphasizes practical application over theoretical studies. An engineering degree in Mechatronics typically takes 4-5 years in India, with master's and PhD programs extending for 2 years each. The field is experiencing significant growth, driven by advancements in robotics, AI, and smart systems.

To succeed in this rapidly evolving field, Mechatronics engineers need to be broad-based professionals, aware of the actual needs and behaviors of users. The curriculum often includes topics such as robotics, industrial automation, control systems, embedded systems, and artificial intelligence.

COURSE DETAILS: HOME ECONOMICS

WHAT DOES A HOME ECONOMICS CAREER ENTAIL?

**Home Economics in India** offers a diverse range of career options related to food, nutrition, housing, clothing, and family resource management. Many home economists work in educational institutions, promoting healthy living and sustainable practices.

Some professionals in this field work in media to promote products. Others conduct research in an area of personal interest in this field. Most home economists supervise at least one other employee.

More specialized aspects include:

**Counselling:** Providing informal guidance to families on managing household income, health, and overall well-being, often through projects, demonstrations, and educational materials.

**Journalism:** Opportunities in print, radio, and television for creative individuals to write articles, host shows, or contribute to columns on topics like cooking, interior design, and family living. Many home economists work as freelance writers or content creators.

Home economists work in a very large variety of work settings. The actual setting is determined by the type, size, location and financial resources of the employer, and by the speciality, experience and education of the home economist. Some positions require evening work and extensive travel.
